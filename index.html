SERVICES

WEB DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

I create custom website designs. This includes the layout and structure of the site, the colour scheme, the graphic details, buttons, etc. I can take your paper napkin sketch, from ketchup smudged idea, through the full development and design process to the finished highly polished website you imagined.

REQUIREMENTS ANLAYSIS

Requirements analysis is critical to the success of any website, system or software project. The requirements should be documented, actionable, measurable, testable, traceable, related to identified business needs or opportunities, and defined to a level of detail sufficient for system design. I can insure you capture your requirements and convert them to actionable tasks and a finished product.

WEBSITE AUDITING & ANALYSIS

For your site to be a success you need to know how people use your website. I can provide an in-depth analysis of your website and its performance and help you understand which areas can be more effective, highlight issues regarding usability, navigation and functionality and provide you with the skills and tools for future monitoring.

CONTENT MANAGEMENT

If you need to have the ability to change your site’s content on a regular basis, I can provide you with that functionality. I can supply custom bespoke functionality or customize Wordpress or Drupal installs to get you up and running quickly and cheaply and provide you with the necessary training to continue to maintain yours site.
